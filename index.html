My name is Litzy Cruz and I am a second year Art History major at the University of California, Riverside. I currently work for the Associated Students Program Board (ASPB) at UCR as the Director of Contemporary Culture. During my first year at UCR, I interned for the Contemporary Culture division.

The Associated Students Program Board focuses on providing quality entertainment for the students at UCR. The program is comprised of six divisions: Concerts, Special Events, Contemporary Culture, Films & Lectures, Marketing, and Membership.

As the Director of Contemporary Culture, I am in charge of programming events that target and highlight important social, political, and cultural ideas that impact UCR students. Some of the recurring events that Contemporary Culture programs are More Than Spoken Word, Winter SOULstice, and Arts Walk.
